PRIVACY POLICY · VERSO

Privacy.

EFFECTIVE 13 MAY 2026 · VERSION 1.0

Verso stores nothing about you off the device. No accounts. No analytics. No tracking. No advertising. No third parties. No financial data ever transmitted.

IN ONE LINE

Your selected cards, monthly budget, recent calculations, and preferences live in your phone's local browser storage — and only there.

What we collect

Nothing. No name, no email, no phone, no location, no device identifier, no usage statistics, no card numbers — nothing leaves your device. Verso is a calculator. It does not connect to any bank or card network.

What we store on your device

Verso uses the browser/WebView's localStorage — a small, app-private store on your phone — to remember:

  • The credit cards you have added to your wallet (only the card name and the bank — never card numbers, CVVs, or any sensitive credentials)
  • Your monthly spending profile for personalised recommendations
  • Recent calculations and split-bill history
  • Theme preference and display settings
  • Optional Google Places API key, if you chose to add one for smart-store detection

This data never leaves your device. If you uninstall Verso, it is deleted with the app. You can also tap Delete all data in the Profile page at any time to wipe it manually.

What about my card numbers?

Verso does not ask for, accept, or store credit card numbers, CVVs, expiry dates, billing addresses, or any card credentials. It only knows the names of the cards (e.g. "DBS Altitude Visa") to look up their rewards rates. It is not a wallet app. It is a calculator.

What network requests Verso makes

By default, none. Verso runs entirely on your device, with rewards rates and bank rules baked into the app itself.

If you choose to enable smart-store detection, Verso will use your own Google Places API key (which you provide) to identify nearby merchants by category. These requests go directly from your device to Google — Verso has no server in between and no access to those requests.

No analytics, no tracking

Verso includes no analytics SDKs, no fingerprinting, no advertising libraries, no crash reporters, no social plugins, no remote configuration. We do not know who you are, when you opened the app, or what you calculated — and that is the design.

Children's privacy

Verso is intended for adults who hold credit cards. We do not knowingly collect any data from anyone — because we do not collect any data at all.

Permissions

On both Android and iOS, Verso requests no special permissions. It does not need access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, photos, or files. The only system facility it uses is local storage on the device itself.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change anything material about how the app handles data, this page will reflect the change and the version + effective date above will update. There is no email list to notify — there is no list.

Contact

For questions, open an issue at github.com/TheMRA99/verso/issues. The source code is public — you can read it line by line to verify everything on this page.
